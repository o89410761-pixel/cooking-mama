# Cooking Preference Questions

## Dietary Preferences

- Do you have any dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, keto, etc.)?
- Any foods you dislike or want to avoid?
- Do you have allergies we should know about?

## Cooking Experience

- How would you rate your cooking skill? (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- Do you prefer simple 15-minute recipes or are you willing to spend time on more complex dishes?

## Cuisine & Flavor

- What cuisines or flavors appeal to you? (Italian, Asian, Mexican, Mediterranean, comfort food, etc.)
- Do you prefer light meals, hearty meals, or a mix?

## Time & Effort

- How much time do you typically have for breakfast/lunch/dinner?
- Do you prefer quick weeknight meals or are you looking for weekend showstoppers?

## Ingredients & Preferences

- Do you cook with fresh ingredients, frozen, pantry staples, or a mix?
- Any ingredients you love and want to see often in recipes?
- Any kitchen equipment limitations (no oven, minimal tools, etc.)?

## Fitness & Body Profile

- **Gender**: Are you male or female? (affects BMR calculation)
- **Age**: Your age in years (needed for accurate BMR)
- **Body profile**: Your approximate weight in kg/lbs and height (helps calculate personalized macro targets)
- **Activity level**: How active are you? (sedentary, lightly active, moderately active, very active, athlete)
- **Training frequency**: How many days per week do you train? (helps assess activity level)
- **Fitness goal**: What are you focused on? (weight loss, muscle gain, maintenance, body recomposition, energy/performance, general health)
- **Macro preferences**: Do you have a preferred macro split? (e.g., high-protein for muscle, low-carb for weight loss, balanced for general health)

## Dietary Goals

- Are you cooking for any specific health goals (weight loss, muscle gain, energy, etc.)?
- Do you prefer protein-heavy, carb-balanced, or fat-forward meals?
- Any daily macro targets you're working toward (e.g., "I want 150g protein per day")?
